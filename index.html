Featured Projects (Minecraft Mods)

Let Your Friend Eating (Minecraft Utility Mod)

A fun and helpful mod that adds the ability for other players to feed you directly, making cooperative play even more engaging!

CurseForge Modrinth GitHub

Stardew Valley Food (Minecraft Content Mod)

Craving some pixelated farm-fresh goodness? This mod introduces a delightful array of delicious food items directly from the beloved game Stardew Valley into your Minecraft world!

CurseForge Modrinth GitHub

Japan Props (Minecraft Decoration Mod)

Transform your builds into beautiful Japanese landscapes! This mod adds a variety of charming and authentic Japanese-style decorative props for all your architectural desires.

CurseForge Modrinth

DropRateAPI (Minecraft API Mod)

A powerful and flexible API designed for developers to easily customize and fine-tune item drop rates within Minecraft, offering endless possibilities for game balance and unique experiences.

CurseForge Website GitHub

Smooth Wood (Minecraft Block Mod)

Say goodbye to jagged edges! This mod introduces a collection of sleek and visually appealing wood blocks with smooth textures, perfect for modern builds and seamless designs.

CurseForge

iRecycle (Minecraft Utility Mod)

Go green in Minecraft! iRecycle is a handy mod that allows you to recycle various items, giving you back resources and promoting sustainability within your blocky world.

CurseForge

Impossible Item Crafting (Minecraft Challenge Mod)

Are you up for a challenge? This mod introduces mind-bending and seemingly "impossible" item crafting recipes, pushing your creative limits and offering a unique twist to your survival experience!

CurseForge

InfoPlus (Minecraft Information Mod)

Never be in the dark again! InfoPlus is a useful client-side mod that provides additional, real-time information about your surroundings, blocks, and entities, enhancing your gameplay knowledge.

Modrinth
